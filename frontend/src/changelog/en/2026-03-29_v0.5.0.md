## Reporting Dashboard

Admins now have a dedicated Reports page under the Administration section. It gives you a bird's-eye view of your organization's activity: total bookings, fill rates, event counts, and active volunteers — all at a glance.

![Reporting dashboard with overview cards, charts, and tables](./images/reporting-dashboard.png)

## Bookings Over Time

A line chart shows confirmed and cancelled bookings across your selected date range. Switch between daily and monthly granularity to spot trends or zoom into specific periods.

## Busiest Times & Top Volunteers

See which hours of the day attract the most bookings and who your most active volunteers are. The top-10 leaderboard makes it easy to recognize dedicated helpers.

## Category & Event Fill Rates

Two detailed tables break down slot capacity and fill rates — first by category, then by individual event. Colored progress bars let you quickly identify which events are fully booked and where there is still room.

## Date Range & CSV Export

Filter all reports by a custom date range and export the data as CSV for further analysis or record-keeping.
